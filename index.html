Parents

When you bring a traditional folktale like Dentist Bird into your home, you open a magical doorway for your child to enter a rich, new world!

  • Folktales teach moral lessons.
  • Folktales expose us to diverse cultures.
  • Folktales spark conversations about problem solving, making friends, and community building.

Are you ready to enter the rich world of Dentist Bird with your child? Download our free Parent’s Guide. It’s full of simple ideas to come together as a family around a unique story, rich art, and great music.
