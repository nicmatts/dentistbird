Parents and Teachers

When you bring a traditional folktale like Dentist Bird into your home or classroom, you open a magical doorway for a child to enter a rich, new world!

Why folktales?

  • Folktales teach moral lessons.
  • Folktales expose us to diverse cultures.
  • Folktales spark conversations about problem solving, making friends, and community building.

For parents
