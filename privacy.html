Dentist Bird Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for the Dentist Bird website and mobile applications.

Information we collect

We don’t request or collect any information about our users or their devices.

We do collect email addresses of interested parties who subscribe to our newsletter. Those email addresses are not shared and are only used for that purpose.

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may ask us to delete your email address from our subscriber list at any time by contacting us via the contact email address on this website.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act

Our mobile applications comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“COPPA”). We don’t knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 years old. If a user is identified as under 13 years old, they will not be permitted to exit the Dentist Bird application to go online to download our teaching materials.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your information for marketing purposes. We do share anonymous information with 3rd parties to help us run and maintain our services.

Security

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.

Wherever we collect sensitive information, that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way.

Updates

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page. This privacy policy was last updated on 12/8/2014.

Questions

If you have any questions about our privacy policies, contact us through our Contact page or by email at info@dentistbird.com.
